Account-Related

Do I need an Avvatar account to be able to shop?

No, you can shop as a guest. However, creating an account helps you track orders and manage details easily.

How can I create my Avvatar account?

You can create an account by clicking on the login icon and selecting “Register”.

How can I update my information if required?

You can update your personal details from the “My Account” section after logging in.

Is my personal information kept private?

Yes, all your personal data is securely stored and never shared with third parties.

I have forgotten my password, how can I reset it?

Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
